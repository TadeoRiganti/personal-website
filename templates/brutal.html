This is a Neo-Brutalist Website.

And it's fucking awesome

You probably think your 15MB parallax homepage with "smooth scrolling" is special. You think your rounded corners and soft gradients make people feel safe. Wrong.

Brutalist web design is the digital equivalent of raw concrete. It doesn't care about your feelings or your "user personas." It rejects the over-optimized, template-driven, corporate bullshit that has turned the internet into a giant, identical shopping mall.

Why this works

  • It's fast as fuck: No heavy animations. No tracking scripts. It loads before you can even blink your judgmental eyes.
  • It's honest: It uses system fonts and default blue links because that’s what a website actually is. It’s text communicating a message.
  • It's responsive by default: You don't need 800 media queries when you aren't trying to force a pixel-perfect layout onto a screen the size of a credit card. It just works.
  • Visible Structure: Why hide the grid? Why hide the borders? Let the user see how the motherfucker is built.

The Reactionary Spirit

This isn't just an aesthetic; it's a middle finger. It's a reaction to the homogenization of the web. While you're busy adding a 100kb jQuery UI library just to animate a background color, Brutalism is actually getting the point across.

"Good design is as little design as possible." — Some German motherfucker.

Stop over-designing. Look at this shit. It's a website. It has content. You can read it. What else do you fucking want?
